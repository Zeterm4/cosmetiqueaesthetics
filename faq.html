Your Questions Answered

We've compiled answers to the most frequently asked questions to help you make informed decisions about your aesthetic journey. If you don't find what you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us directly.

What makes Cosmetique Aesthetics different from other clinics?

+

At Cosmetique Aesthetics, we combine medical expertise with aesthetic artistry under the leadership of Dr. Yekaterina Khronusova. Our unique approach focuses on both wellness and beauty, offering comprehensive treatments from non-invasive procedures to surgical interventions. We prioritize natural-looking results and personalized care for each patient.

What are your clinic hours?

+

We are open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. We also offer extended hours for certain procedures by appointment.

Do you accept insurance?

+

Most aesthetic treatments are considered elective and are not covered by insurance. However, some medical treatments may be covered. We recommend checking with your insurance provider. We offer various financing options including CareCredit and Cherry to make treatments more accessible.

What should I expect during my first visit?

+

Your first visit will include a comprehensive consultation where we discuss your goals, medical history, and treatment options. Dr. Khronusova will examine the treatment area and create a personalized treatment plan. We'll also discuss costs, timeline, and answer any questions you may have.

How long do injectable treatments last?

+

Results vary by treatment type and individual factors. Botox typically lasts 3-4 months, while dermal fillers can last 6-18 months depending on the product used and treatment area. We'll discuss expected duration during your consultation and recommend a maintenance schedule.

Are your treatments painful?

+

Most of our treatments involve minimal discomfort. We use topical numbing creams, ice, and advanced injection techniques to maximize comfort. For more extensive procedures, we offer various pain management options to ensure your comfort throughout the treatment.

When will I see results from my treatment?

+

Results timing varies by treatment. Botox results appear within 3-7 days, dermal fillers show immediate results that improve over 2 weeks, and skin treatments may take several weeks to show full benefits. We'll provide specific timelines for your chosen treatments.

What is the difference between surgical and non-surgical treatments?

+

Non-surgical treatments require little to no downtime and provide subtle, gradual improvements. Surgical procedures offer more dramatic results but require longer recovery periods. During consultation, we'll help you determine which approach best suits your goals and lifestyle.

How do I schedule a consultation?

+

You can schedule a consultation by calling us at (702) 555-0123, using our online booking system, or filling out our contact form. We typically can accommodate consultations within 1-2 weeks of your request.

Is there a consultation fee?

+

We charge a consultation fee of $150, which is applied toward any treatment you decide to proceed with on the same day. This ensures quality time with Dr. Khronusova to discuss your goals and create a personalized treatment plan.

What should I bring to my consultation?

+

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card (if applicable), list of current medications, and any relevant medical records. It's also helpful to bring photos of your desired results or areas of concern to help us understand your goals.

Can I get treatment on the same day as my consultation?

+

In many cases, yes! For non-surgical treatments like injectables, we can often provide treatment immediately following consultation if you're a good candidate. Surgical procedures require a separate appointment to allow for proper preparation and planning.

What should I expect after treatment?

+

Post-treatment experiences vary by procedure. Common temporary effects may include mild swelling, redness, or bruising. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and are available for any questions or concerns during your recovery period.

When can I return to work after treatment?

+

Most non-surgical treatments allow immediate return to work. Some may require 1-2 days if there's visible swelling or bruising. Surgical procedures vary from several days to weeks depending on the extent of the procedure. We'll discuss specific timelines during consultation.

Are there any activities I should avoid after treatment?

+

Post-treatment restrictions depend on the procedure. Generally, we recommend avoiding strenuous exercise, excessive heat (saunas, hot tubs), and alcohol for 24-48 hours after injectable treatments. We provide comprehensive aftercare instructions tailored to your specific treatment.

What if I'm not satisfied with my results?

+

Patient satisfaction is our priority. If you have concerns about your results, please contact us immediately. We offer follow-up appointments to assess results and make adjustments when possible. Our goal is to ensure you're completely happy with your outcome.

What financing options do you offer?

+

We partner with CareCredit and Cherry to offer flexible financing options. These programs provide monthly payment plans with various terms and interest rates. We also accept cash, credit cards, and offer package deals for multiple treatments.

How do I apply for financing?

+

You can apply for CareCredit or Cherry financing online or in our office. The application process is quick, and you'll receive an instant decision in most cases. Our staff can help you through the application process and explain the terms.

Do you offer package deals or discounts?

+

Yes! We offer package deals for multiple treatments and maintenance programs. We also have seasonal promotions and loyalty programs for returning patients. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about current offers.

What is your payment policy?

+

Payment is due at the time of service unless you're using an approved financing plan. We accept cash, credit cards, and financing through our approved partners. For surgical procedures, we may require a deposit to secure your appointment date.
